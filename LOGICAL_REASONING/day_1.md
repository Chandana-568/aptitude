# 📝 Logical Reasoning Practice – Day 01 (Answers)

## Q1. Number Series
**Question:** 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, ?

**Answer:** 64

**Explanation:** Each number is multiplied by 2.
2 × 2 = 4
4 × 2 = 8
8 × 2 = 16
16 × 2 = 32
32 × 2 = **64**

## Q2. Odd One Out
**Question:** Square, Rectangle, Circle, Triangle

**Answer:** Circle

**Explanation:** Circle has no sides or vertices, while the others are polygons.

## Q3. Coding-Decoding
**Question:** If DOG → EPH, then CAT → ?

**Answer:** DBU

**Explanation:** Move each letter one step forward.
C → D
A → B
T → U

## Q4. Blood Relation
**Question:** A is the brother of B. B is the sister of C. How is A related to C?

**Answer:** Brother

**Explanation:** Since A and B are siblings, and B and C are siblings, A is also C's brother.

## Q5. Direction Sense

**Answer:** North-East

**Explanation:** The final position is towards the north-east of the starting point.

## Q9. Direction Sense
**Question:** A person walks 10 m north, then 10 m east, then 10 m south. In which direction is the person from the starting point?

**Answer:** East

**Explanation:** The north and south movements cancel each other, leaving the person 10 m east of the starting point.

## Q15. Blood Relation
**Question:** A is the father of B. B is the sister of C. C is the father of D. How is A related to D?

**Answer:** Grandfather

**Explanation:** A is the father of C, and C is the father of D, so A is D's grandfather.

## Q16. Direction Sense
**Question:** A man walks 5 km east, then 5 km north, then 5 km west. In which direction is he from the starting point?

**Answer:** North

**Explanation:** The east and west movements cancel each other, leaving him 5 km north of the starting point.

## Q17. Statement & Conclusion

**Statement:**
All flowers are plants.
Rose is a flower.

**Answer:** A) Only Conclusion 1 follows.

**Explanation:** Since every flower is a plant and rose is a flower, rose is a plant. The second conclusion is incorrect.

---

## Q18. Analogy
**Question:** Book : Read :: Food : ?

**Answer:** Eat

**Explanation:** A book is read, and food is eaten.

---

## Q19. Calendar
**Question:** If today is Tuesday, what day will it be after 10 days?

**Answer:** Friday

**Explanation:** 10 days = 7 days + 3 days.
Tuesday + 3 days = Friday.

---

## Q20. Mirror Image
**Question:** Name one English alphabet that remains unchanged in a vertical mirror.

**Answer:** A

**Explanation:** Letters such as A, H, I, M, O, T, U, V, W, X, and Y have vertical symmetry.
