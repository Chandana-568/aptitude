# Aptitude Practice – Day 1

**Total Questions:** 25

---

## Quantitative Aptitude

### 1. Percentage

**Question:** A shirt's price is increased by 20% and then decreased by 20%. What is the overall percentage change?

**Answer:** 4% decrease

---

### 2. Profit & Loss

**Question:** A shopkeeper buys an article for ₹800 and sells it for ₹920. Find the profit percentage.

**Answer:** 15%

---

### 3. Ratio & Proportion

**Question:** The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 5:3. If there are 40 boys, how many girls are there?

**Answer:** 24 girls

---

### 4. Simple Interest

**Question:** Find the simple interest on ₹5000 at 8% per annum for 3 years.

**Answer:** ₹1200

---

### 5. Average

**Question:** Find the average of 8, 10, 12, 14, and 16.

**Answer:** 12

---

### 6. Time & Work

**Question:** A can complete a piece of work in 12 days and B can complete it in 18 days. In how many days can they complete the work together?

**Answer:** 7.2 days

---

### 7. Time, Speed & Distance

**Question:** A car travels 180 km in 3 hours. Find its speed.

**Answer:** 60 km/h

---

### 8. Number System

**Question:** Find the HCF of 24 and 36.

**Answer:** 12

### 9. Ages

**Question:** The present age of a father is 40 years and his son is 12 years. After how many years will the father's age be twice the son's age?
