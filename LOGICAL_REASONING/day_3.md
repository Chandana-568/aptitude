# Day 3 - Aptitude & Logical Reasoning

## Number System

### Q1. Find the HCF of 48, 72, and 96.
**Answer:** 12

### Q2. Find the LCM of 18 and 24.
**Answer:** 72

### Q3. A number leaves a remainder of 5 when divided by 12. What remainder will be left when twice the number is divided by 12?
**Answer:** 10

### Q4. The product of two numbers is 540 and their HCF is 6. Find their LCM.
**Answer:** 90

### Q5. Which of the following is divisible by 9?
- 4527
- 5638
- 7215
- 8143

**Answer:** 4527

## Percentage

### Q6. What is 35% of 480?
**Answer:** 168

### Q7. A student's marks increased from 400 to 460. Find the percentage increase.
**Answer:** 15%

### Q8. The price of a shirt is increased by 20% and then decreased by 20%. What is the net percentage change?
**Answer:** 4% decrease

### Q9. If 45% of a number is 180, find the number.
**Answer:** 400

### Q10. In an election, Candidate A got 60% of the votes. If the total votes were 2500, how many votes did Candidate B receive?
**Answer:** 1000

## Profit & Loss

### Q11. A shopkeeper buys an article for ₹800 and sells it for ₹920. Find the profit percentage.
**Answer:** 15%

### Q12. An item is sold for ₹675 at a loss of 10%. Find its cost price.
**Answer:** ₹750

### Q13. A trader marks an article 25% above cost price and gives a discount of 10%. Find the profit percentage.
**Answer:** 12.5%

### Q14. A product is bought for ₹450 and sold for ₹495. Find the gain percentage.
**Answer:** 10%

### Q15. A man sold a watch for ₹960 at a profit of 20%. Find the cost price.
**Answer:** ₹800

## Average

### Q16. Find the average of 24, 36, 48, 60 and 72.
**Answer:** 48

### Q17. The average of 8 numbers is 42. If one number is removed, the average becomes 40. Find the removed number.
**Answer:** 56

### Q18. The average age of 5 friends is 24 years. A new friend joins, making the average 25 years. Find the age of the new friend.
**Answer:** 30 years

### Q19. The average of first 15 natural numbers is?
**Answer:** 8

### Q20. The average of 12 numbers is 36. If each number is increased by 4, what will be the new average?
**Answer:** 40

## Logical Reasoning

### Q21. Number Series

3, 7, 15, 31, 63, ?

**Answer:** 127

### Q22. Coding-Decoding

If BOOK is written as CPPL, how is PEN written?

**Answer:** QFO

### Q23. Blood Relation

Pointing to a woman, Ravi said, "She is the daughter of my mother's only son."

Who is the woman to Ravi?

**Answer:** Daughter

### Q24. Direction Sense

A person walks 8 km North, then 6 km East, then 8 km South. In which direction is he from the starting point?

**Answer:** East

### Q25. Analogy

Doctor : Hospital :: Teacher : ?

**Answer:** School
